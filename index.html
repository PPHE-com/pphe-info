About Us

PPHE (Promoting Personal Hygiene Environmentally) is an initiative that aims to improve the hygiene of low-income families in coastal Kenya. In short, the project takes advantage of the single-use soaps at hotels in Mombasa which are typically discarded after every guest. Instead, we can work with major hotels to repurpose these soaps so that they benefit marginalized communities.

The soap pieces undergo a chemical cleansing with an alcohol-based sanitizer, they are then reprocessed through grinding and plodded into new bars. We intend to add natural mosquito repellents into the soap product to act as a malaria safeguard improving the overall product. Currently, we have distributed 300 soap bars to communties in Kwale and have an additional 75KG of used soap that will be rebatched in October.
